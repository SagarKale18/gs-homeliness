About the workshop

One trusted name for every room in the building.

GS Homeliness has been operating privately since 2018. In 2026, the company grew into a leading manufacturer of premium furniture for homes and offices through new and expanded partnerships.

Whether you're updating a single room or planning a full interior, we help you build spaces that are comfortable, organised and built to last — sofas, recliners, beds, dining sets, study furniture and storage, alongside modular kitchens, wardrobes and TV units, all under one Indian furniture brand.

Every piece moves between two traditions we hold equally: the intricacy of handcrafted solid wood, and the precision of modular, flat-pack design.

Fifteen years in

Trust that was earned one installation at a time.

With over 15 years of experience, the workshop has earned the trust and loyalty of its customers through quality craftsmanship and skilled artisans — delivering home furniture, office furniture and hotel interiors alike.

We're recognised for attractive Indian-style design and premium teak-wood work, including traditional Deoghar-style furniture built the way it's meant to be built.
